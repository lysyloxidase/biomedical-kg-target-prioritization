# Osteoarthritis Biology Ground Truth

Disease anchor: knee osteoarthritis is represented by `EFO_0004616`
(`MONDO:0005416`, ICD10 `M17`, MeSH `D020370`).

## Known Genes For Sanity Checks

- Anabolic and chondroprotective: `GDF5`, `SOX9`, `ACAN`, `COL2A1`, `FRZB`
- Catabolic extracellular matrix: `MMP13`, `ADAMTS5`, `ADAMTS4`
- Cytokines and pain: `IL1B`, `IL6`, `NGF`
- GWAS hits: `GDF5`, `FRZB`, `COL11A1`, `COL27A1`, `ALDH1A2`, `ASTN2`,
  `FTO`, `GLT8D1`, `MCF2L`, `TGFB1`, `IL11`, `SMO`

## Pathways

Relevant pathway families include TGF-beta/BMP, Wnt/beta-catenin, NF-kappaB,
MAPK, IL-1/IL-6 signaling, cartilage extracellular matrix and collagen biology,
and senescence/SASP.

## Genetics References

- Hatzikotoulas et al. Nature 2025, DOI `10.1038/s41586-025-08771-z`: all-OA
  genetics; use knee-OA Open Targets associations where available.
- Boer et al. Cell 2021, DOI `10.1016/j.cell.2021.07.038`: OA risk variants
  across 11 phenotypes.

## Clinical Caveat

No disease-modifying OA drug is approved. Drugs discussed in case studies, such
as lorecivivint, sprifermin, tanezumab, MIV-711, lutikizumab, GLPG1972, and
UBX0101, are evidence anchors rather than validated benchmark answers.
